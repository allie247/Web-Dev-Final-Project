Shopping

Burlington is a one of a kind when it comes to shopping, with a diverse group of local stores just downtown!

Vermont Gift Barn

This traditional gift shop showcases a wide range of local arts and crafts, including apparel, jewelry, pottery, maple syrups, and more!

Vintage Inspired Marketplace

With over 50 vendors, this multi-dealer shop offers an always-changing selection of vintage goods, including artwork, clothing, and collectibles.

Homeport

Occupying 4 floors, this store offers an extensive range of products to meet your household needs, featuring furniture, kitchen gadgets, gardening supplies, and more.

Saratoga Olive Oil Company

This family owned business sources high quality olive oils from across the globe, offering a variety of flavored cooking oils and vinaigrettes.

Home and Garden Vermont

Located just off of Church Street, this brick-and-mortar shop specializes in selling garden and nature inspired goods and fresh flower bouquets.
