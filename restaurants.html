Restaurants

Burlington is home to an eclectic restaurant scene, with an emphasis on serving local Vermont fare.

Henry’s Diner

This classic American diner is the perfect breakfast or brunch destination, serving specialty skillets, pancakes, benedicts, and more!

HANA Japanese Restaurant

This spot offers an extensive selection of high quality sushi rolls, hibachi, and other Japanese style dishes for an incredible price!

Leunig’s Bistro

This upscale restaurant combines French dishes with local Vermont fare, serving a wide variety of eclectic and seasonal cocktails, appetizers, and entrees.

Zabby and Elf’s Stone Soup

This cafe accentuates comfort and soul with their beautifully created pastries, soups, and sandwiches to establish the perfect lunch spot.

Waterworks Food and Drink

Established in a historic riverside mill, this upscale restaurant offers unique cocktails and a wide selection of new American entrees.
