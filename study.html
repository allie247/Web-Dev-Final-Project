Study Spots

Having a nice place to get work and studying done is a very important part of being a student at Univeristy of Vermont.

On Campus:

  • Howe Library: the largest of the three libraries on campus with three floor, getting quieter with each level. The first floor is a great place to get group work done or chat with friends. The second and third floor is a great place to get studying done on your own.
  • Hendersons: a coffee shop in the Davis Center on campus. This is a busier atmosphere for people who like to work with background noise.
  • Billings Library: this is a smaller library on campus with a historical feel.

Off Campus

  • Fletcher: this is a public library downtown, for the times you need to take a walk and get away from campus.
  • Kru Coffee: Coffee shop at the top of Church Street downtown Burlington. They have great coffee and food and comfortable seating.
