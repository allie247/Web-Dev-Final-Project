Night Life in Burlington VT

Church Street is a great lit up place to spend your evenings into night time going out for drinks, food, or dessert!

Bars!

  • Akes place: Akes in decribed as a "Rock and Roll Sports Bar" located in the middle of Chruch Street that has been open since 1990.
  • Lincoln's: a bar "hidden" on College Street, right off of Church, that resembles a tavern. This is a cash only bar and most of the drinks are just $5!
  • Rira's Irish Pub: serving food during the day and a fun scene for dancing during the night, located on Church Street.

Late Night Snacks!

  • Insomnia Cookies: late night cookie shop that is open until 1am.
  • Little Gordo Creemee Stand: a seasonal icecream stand near the downtown area. This small window will create a huge line down South Union Street in the summer time.
  • Mr Mikes Pizza: a great place to grab a slice of pizza, watch tv, and play a game of pool.
